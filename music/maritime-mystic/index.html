about

gravemint's vocaloid debut, featuring synthesizer performances from miku and luka with full instrumental versions of every track. inspired by the beauty of the pacific northwest and the puget sound, maritime mystic explores the loneliness and wonder of arriving somewhere new, far from everyone you know. sparkly synths, harps, piano, and clean bass weave through themes of solitude, mystery, and the strange pull of an unfamiliar coast
