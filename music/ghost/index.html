about

a glitchy, chiptune-adjacent experiment where nearly every instrument is christian's own voice, heavily modulated and transformed beyond recognition. about how completely you can believe something in the moment, and how foreign that feeling can look later. heavy beat, manipulated vocal layers, and a restless, distorted energy throughout
