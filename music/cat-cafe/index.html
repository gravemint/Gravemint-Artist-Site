about

a two-sided release inspired by a cat cafe in downtown seattle, capturing the full duality of cats themselves. one version is a breakneck, glitch-adjacent track built on fast experimental beats and piano; the other a calm, jazz-inflected piece filled with ambient purring and warm atmosphere. two moods, one idea: the creature that knocks things off shelves and then curls up in your lap
