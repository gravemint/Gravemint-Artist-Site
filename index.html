about

sounds from somewhere between a forest and a dream

gravemint is a michigan-based producer who has been making melodically driven electronic music since 2017. built from ethereal, reverb-laden synths, harps, pianos, ghostly vocal fragments, and textures often drawn from the natural world, the music explores themes of the transcendent, the mystical, and the ineffable

the sound of gravemint grew out of a wide range of influences. rock and metal early on, then edm and pop, and eventually everything from tycho to porter robinson
