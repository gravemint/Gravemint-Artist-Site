about

sounds from somewhere between a forest and a dream

gravemint is a michigan-based producer who has been making melodically driven electronic music since 2017. built from ethereal, reverb-laden synths, harps, and ghostly vocal fragments, he aims to make music that feels closer to a dream than a song

the sound of gravemint grew out of a wide range of influences. rock and metal early on, then edm and pop, and eventually artists as varied as tycho, porter robinson, and grimes
